Lock in your first students before the doors open. DM 30–40 people directly — friends, family, anyone who's ever asked Zey about yoga. Offer them a founding member rate: MVR 2,000 for the first month only, locked in forever as long as they stay. This guarantees the first class isn't half-empty, gets real money in before launch day, and gives the business momentum from the very first session.
