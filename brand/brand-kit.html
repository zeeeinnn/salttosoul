The Brand
Rooted in salt.
Rising toward soul.

Drawing inspiration from Zey's grounded, nature-forward personality and 15 years in the Maldivian hospitality industry, we developed a brand identity that reflects her authentic transformation from the pace of luxury resort life to the stillness of the yoga mat. The design embraces her holistic vision and the deep connection she carries between body, breath, and the island world she comes from.

The result is a brand that feels both ancient and intimate approachable in its warmth, unhurried in its tone, and unmistakably Maldivian in its roots. Every element is crafted to speak to anyone who has ever felt the pull between doing and being, and chosen, finally, to pause.

Usage Rules
Do and don't.
Do
Use the mark on approved brand backgrounds only Coconut Night, Terracotta, Blush, Fern, or Driftwood
Keep clear space around the logo equal to the height of the spiral mark on all sides
Use the full mark as one unit the spiral and centre are always together, never separated
Use Cormorant Garamond for all headlines and display text, Josefin Sans for body
Use photography that feels quiet, honest, and naturally lit real moments over posed ones
Don't
Don't stretch, rotate, skew, or distort the mark in any direction
Don't place the logo on busy photography or against low-contrast backgrounds
Don't add drop shadows, glows, outlines, or graphic effects to the mark
Don't use off-brand colours or swap in other typefaces the warmth of the palette is deliberate
Don't recreate or redraw the icons always use the original files from this kit

Voice & Tone
How Salt to Soul speaks.
Grounded
No toxic positivity. Acknowledge the hard parts that's exactly where the trust is built. Speak to the whole person, not the highlight reel.
Intimate
Speak to one person, not a crowd. Write as if you're sharing something quietly over tea. Not announcing. Just telling.
Maldivian
This is rooted in where you come from the ocean, the warmth, the light. That specificity is your advantage. Own it completely.
Unhurried
The brand breathes slowly. Short sentences. White space. Silence between the words. Resist the urge to fill every moment with content.
Honest
Share the real the doubt before the breakthrough, the sore body, the homesick mornings in Bali. Authenticity earns the people who will show up.
Inviting
Every post leaves room for the reader. A question. An open door. This brand isn't a performance it's a conversation.

Content categories
The Journey
Raw moments from Bali arrivals, breakthroughs, the hard days. Builds the trust that turns followers into clients.
The Practice
Yoga, breathwork, meditation. Teach something small a cue, a pose, one breath technique. Show the expertise.
The Words
Quotes that found you, a reflection, one line that hit differently. Use the quote post template from this kit.
The Place
Bali the light, the temple, the rice field at 6am. Let people feel where you are and why it matters.
The Return
From week 3 onward tease what's coming. Classes, sessions, dates. Build the waitlist before you land.
Bali posting cadence
Stories
Daily one or two. Morning practice, a training moment, a thought before bed. Low effort, high connection.
Feed Posts
3–4 per week. Quality over frequency. If it doesn't feel right for the brand, save it for a story instead.
Reels
Once a week if possible. Candid clips from practice or Bali life. Genuine always beats polished.
The Waitlist
Start teasing return classes by week 3. Ask people to DM "classes" to be first to know when you're back.
