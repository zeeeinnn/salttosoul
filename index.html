Hi, I'm

Zey

Ten years in the Maldivian hospitality industry taught me how to care for others, move through pressure with grace, and find beauty in small moments. The sea was always there, glistening everywhere I looked. Always moving, always returning to itself. Like a dance.

The sea was always teaching me stillness. I just took a long time to listen.

Life brought me to places I never expected. The people I met and the experiences that shaped me slowly brought me closer to myself. Over time I learned to appreciate solitude, protect my peace, and find beauty in simply being.

Yoga came to me as a return. To breath, to softness, to a quiet kind of strength. What began with movement became something deeper. Meditation, healing, and self-inquiry. Eventually a decision that surprised everyone who knew me. I quit. And that path led me to Bali.

Salt to Soul grew from there. Rooted in island life, shaped by the warmth of the Maldivian sun and the rhythm of the ocean. The belief that healing is about becoming more yourself.
